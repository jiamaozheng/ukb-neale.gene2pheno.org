S-PrediXcan associations based on Neale Lab’s UKB GWAS results


Following Neale Lab’s lead on open science and open data sharing, we are releasing the bulk runs of PrediXcan association using Neale Lab's UKB gwas summary results and 44 tissues models from GTEx V6 p release.


NOTICE: Please keep in mind that these are preliminary results provided for your convenience. They will be updated as newer analysis are performed. Date will be used as version.

NOTICE: We are moving files around to improve performance of the webpage. We appreciate your patience.

We are releasing all PrediXcan associations based on Neale Lab's UKB rapid GWAS results

As many of you may know, Neale Lab made a big splash in the GWAS community by releasing the summary results of 2400+ phenotypes from the UK Biobank. Following their lead on open science and open data sharing, we are releasing the bulk runs of PrediXcan association based on Neale Lab’s UKB results and 44 tissues models from GTEx V6p release.
